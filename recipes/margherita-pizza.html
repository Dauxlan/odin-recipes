Pizza Margherita

Pizza Margherita served with basil and sour cream

This pizza recipe can serve up to a theoretically infinite amount of people given enough ingredients.

However this pizza will be so good that the infinite might as well be finite™©

 

without further ado here is the recipe you've been waiting for:

Ingredients

  • pizza base
  • cheese
  • pizza sauce
  • basil
  • sour cream
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  1. put sauce on base as needed
  2. toss chunks of cheese onto pizza base as needed
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. oven at 400°F until pizza is done, good luck
  5. add the basil and sour cream for aesthetic
